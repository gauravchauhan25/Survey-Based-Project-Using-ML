# Data Privacy Awareness and Cyber Law Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the awareness of data privacy, cybersecurity practices, and cyber laws among students. The study aims to understand how students perceive personal data protection, online privacy risks, and legal frameworks related to digital security.

The project combines **survey analysis, AI/ML techniques, NLP-based text processing, data visualization, and web development** to create an interactive and informative platform for spreading awareness about data privacy and user rights.

---

# 🎯 Objectives of the Project

- To analyze the level of data privacy awareness among students and understand how well they know about their personal data usage.
- To study the understanding of major data protection laws like the Information Technology Act and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act.
- To identify common risks, challenges, and misconceptions related to online data usage.
- To apply AI/ML and NLP techniques to analyze subjective responses collected through the survey.
- To visualize the results using Power BI dashboards for better clarity and interpretation.
- To develop a user-friendly website to present insights and spread awareness about data privacy and user rights.
